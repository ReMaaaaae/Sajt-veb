About us

Welcome to the web presentation of Energy Saving Group, a leading private company in United States, which provides consulting services in the fields of renewable energy sources, energy efficiency, restructuring of the energy sector, formation of a free energy market, definition of energy development strategy, development and planning of power systems, definition tariff systems and energy price policy.

We prepare feasibility studies, business plans and other techno-economic and tender documentation for the construction of energy facilities. We define and carry out projects for the improvement of energy efficiency in industry, and we are also a flexible engineering and project bureau that deals with the reconstruction and construction of complex energy facilities and the introduction of automatic control in industrial processes.

ESG is a company that makes its profits by enabling its clients to achieve much greater savings and/or profits at the same time.
